AIRBRUSH MAKEUP

Airbrush makeup is makeup sprayed
onto the skin using an airbrush machine
instead of being applied with sponges,
brushes, fingers, or other methods.

HYDRAFACIAL TREATMENT

A HydraFacial is a non-invasive skin resurfacing treatment
that can be used to treat a variety of skin conditions,
including acne, dryness, and wrinkles.

BODY POLISHING

Body polishing is a beauty treatment that involves
exfoliating the skin to remove dead skin cells,
dirt, and impurities. The process leaves your
skin looking brighter, smoother, and more even.

In threading, a thin cotton or polyester thread is doubled, then twisted. It is then rolled over areas of unwanted hair, plucking the hair at the follicle level. Waxing is the process of hair removal from the root by using a covering of a sticky substance, such as wax, to adhere to body hair, and then removing this covering and pulling out the hair from the follicle.

NAIL EXTENSIONS

Nail extensions are a technique that involves adding
artificial enhancements to natural nails to make them
longer, stronger, and more durable. They can also help
people achieve their desired nail shape and design.

Thermal heat styling temporarily changes the texture of hair strands using heat.
